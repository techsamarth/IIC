About IIC & Summit

The Institution's Innovation Council (IIC) at NIT Durgapur is dedicated to fostering a culture of innovation and entrepreneurship among students and faculty. Our mission is to create an ecosystem that encourages creative thinking, problem-solving, and the development of innovative solutions to real-world challenges.

The Startup Summit 2025 is our flagship event, bringing together aspiring entrepreneurs, industry experts, investors, and innovators from across the country. This summit provides a platform for networking, knowledge sharing, and showcasing groundbreaking ideas that have the potential to transform industries and create positive impact.

Join us for an immersive experience featuring keynote sessions, workshops, pitch competitions, and mentorship opportunities designed to empower the next generation of startup founders.

Event Highlights

Pitch Battle
